About Us

Waikiki is a prominent Turkish fashion retail brand known for offering affordable and stylish clothing, accessories, and home products. Founded in 1988 by the French company Louis-Charles in France, it was later acquired by the Turkish company Taha Group in 1997 and subsequently expanded its operations in Turkey and beyond.
